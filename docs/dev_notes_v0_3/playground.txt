Goal: Make creative iteration frictionless.

Ideas

“Director Mode”: Control scenes frame-by-frame like a movie timeline.

Emotion Rewrite Prompts: “Make this scene sadder” automatically adjusts lighting, music, dialogue tone.

Prop Injection Prompting: “Add a coffee cup on the table” adds object layer automatically.

Scene Replay Mode: Quickly play through multiple dialogue branches to test flow.

Undo Stack for Prompts: Revert Playground edits with natural-language “Undo last change.”
